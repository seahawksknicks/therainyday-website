Delete Your Account

Last Updated: April 21, 2026

The short version: You can delete your Libations account at any time from inside the app, or by emailing us. All of your personal data is removed within 30 days.

Delete from Inside the App

This is the fastest way to delete your account. The option is available on both iOS and Android:

  1. Open the Libations app on your device.
  2. Sign in to your account if you aren't already.
  3. Tap the Profile tab at the bottom.
  4. Tap Settings (gear icon in the top-right).
  5. Scroll to the bottom of the screen and tap Delete Account.
  6. Confirm the action. Your account and all associated data will be deleted immediately.

Delete by Email

If you can no longer sign in to the app — for example, because you lost access to your email address — you can request deletion by contacting us.

Send an email to contact@therainyday.co from the email address associated with your account, with the subject line "Delete My Account".

We will process the request within 7 business days. We may ask for additional information to verify you own the account before deleting it.

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, we permanently remove the following from our systems:

  • Your email address and display name
  • Your entire drink library, including all ratings, tasting notes, photos, and wish list
  • Your taste profile and preferences
  • Your friend connections
  • Your subscription status on our servers (see note below about Google Play)
  • All authentication tokens and session data

What We Keep

We may retain a small amount of data after your account is deleted, but only where we are required to by law or for a legitimate business reason:

  • Financial transaction records — required for tax and accounting purposes. This is limited to the payment amount, date, and an anonymized user ID. We do not retain your email address or personal details here.
  • Anonymized analytics events — general app usage statistics that are not tied to your identity and cannot be traced back to you.

All personal data tied to your identity is deleted or fully anonymized within 30 days of your account deletion request.

Subscriptions

Deleting your account does not automatically cancel your active Libations Premium subscription, because subscriptions are managed by Apple or Google, not by us.

To cancel your subscription:

  • iOS: Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → Libations → Cancel Subscription
  • Android: Google Play Store → Menu → Subscriptions → Libations → Cancel

We recommend canceling your subscription before deleting your account.

Questions?

If you have any questions about account deletion or data retention, email us at contact@therainyday.co. For full details on how we handle your data, see our Privacy Policy.
