Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 26, 2026  ·  Last Updated: March 15, 2026

The short version: We collect only what's needed to run the app. We don't sell your data. We don't show ads. Your drink library and personal preferences are yours.

1. Who We Are

This Privacy Policy applies to Libations and any other apps or services ("Services") provided by The Rainy Day Company ("Company," "we," "us," or "our"), reachable at contact@therainyday.co and operated through therainyday.co.

By using our Services, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this Policy.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your email address and the display name you choose. We use this to identify you, enable login, and allow features like sharing recommendations with other users.

Your Drink Library

The core of the app is your personal drink journal. We store everything you add to it: drink names, categories, ratings, tasting notes, photos, and your "want to try" wish list. This data is associated with your account and kept as long as your account exists.

Taste Profile

You may optionally fill out a taste profile, including flavor preferences, style preferences (e.g., cocktails, scotch, wine), ingredient exclusions, and related settings. This information is used solely to personalize AI recommendations.

Photos

If you upload photos of drinks, those images are stored in our cloud storage provider. They are linked to your drink entries and your account. You can delete any photo at any time by editing or removing the associated drink entry.

Location Data

If you use the location feature to tag where you had a drink, we request access to your device's location to suggest nearby venues. Location access is optional and only active when you use the feature. We store the venue names you select but do not continuously track your position.

AI Recommendation Prompts

When you use the AI recommendation feature, your text prompt and relevant portions of your taste profile and drink history are sent to our AI provider (Anthropic) to generate a response. We store your most recent recommendation prompt for convenience (to pre-populate the next session). We do not use your prompts to train AI models.

Usage Data

We may collect basic, non-identifiable technical information such as error logs and crash reports to diagnose and fix issues. We do not track detailed behavioral analytics.

Device & Technical Information

Standard technical metadata (device type, operating system version, app version) may be collected by our infrastructure providers in the normal course of operating the Services.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the Services — storing your library, generating recommendations, enabling account access.
  • To improve the Services — diagnosing errors, fixing bugs, and improving reliability.
  • To communicate with you — responding to support requests, sending essential service notifications (e.g., billing updates).
  • To process payments — if you subscribe to a premium plan, payment is handled by Apple (App Store) or our payment processor. We do not store full payment card information.

We do not use your data for advertising, we do not sell or rent your personal information, and we do not use it to build advertising profiles.

4. Information Shared with Third Parties

We use a limited number of trusted third-party service providers to operate the Services. Each receives only the data necessary for their specific function:

Supabase

Our database and authentication infrastructure. Your account data, drink library, and uploaded photos are stored on Supabase servers. Supabase processes data on our behalf and is bound by a Data Processing Agreement. Supabase Privacy Policy →

Anthropic

Our AI provider (Claude). When you request a recommendation, relevant context from your profile and library is sent to Anthropic's API to generate the response. Anthropic's API usage is governed by their usage policies and does not use API inputs to train their models. Anthropic Privacy Policy →

Vercel

Our web hosting provider. If you access Libations through a web browser, standard web traffic data (IP addresses, request logs) passes through Vercel's infrastructure. Vercel Privacy Policy →

Apple

If you download the iOS app via the App Store or subscribe through Apple's in-app purchase system, Apple processes your purchase information under their own privacy policy. Apple Privacy Policy →

We do not share your personal information with any other third parties without your explicit consent, except as required by law.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it by law (e.g., financial transaction records for tax purposes).

You may delete your account at any time from within the app (Profile → Preferences → Delete Account), or by emailing contact@therainyday.co.

6. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the following rights with respect to your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Deletion — request deletion of your data and account.
  • Portability — request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Restriction — request that we limit how we process your data in certain circumstances.
  • Opt-out of sale (California / CCPA) — we do not sell personal information; this right is automatically satisfied.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at contact@therainyday.co. We will respond within 30 days (or as required by applicable law).

7. Age Requirement

Libations is an app designed for tracking and discovering alcoholic beverages. Our Services are intended solely for users who are 21 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 21. If you believe someone under 21 has created an account, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

8. Data Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including encrypted transmission (HTTPS/TLS), access controls, and row-level security in our database. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Governing Law

By using our Services, you consent to this transfer. Where required by law (e.g., GDPR), we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

If the changes are significant, we will notify you by email or through a notice in the app. Continued use of the Services after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
